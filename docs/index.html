Aviary for reMarkable — Privacy Policy

Effective February 27, 2026

The Aviary iOS app is built by Mitchell Scott. This policy describes how the app handles your data.

Overview

Aviary does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to the developer.

The app allows users to send files and URLs from the iOS Share Sheet to an Aviary backend server configured and controlled by the user. All data transmission occurs directly between the device and the user's chosen server.

The app does not use analytics, tracking technologies, advertising, or third-party SDKs. No data is sold or shared with third parties.

What Is Stored on Your Device

  • Your server URL (stored in UserDefaults)
  • Your authentication token (stored in the iOS Keychain)
  • An offline upload queue of pending files (temporary; deleted after successful upload)

Permissions

Aviary may request permission to send notifications. Notifications are used solely to inform you of upload status.

Backend Server

Any data processed by the backend server is governed by the privacy practices of the server operator.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.
